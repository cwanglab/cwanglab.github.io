## Publication Philosophy

**Quality over Quantity:**

We prioritize high-impact publications over publication count. We aim for:

- Top-tier venues (Nature family, IEEE TPAMI, CVPR, ICML, MICCAI)
- Clinical relevance and real-world validation
- Reproducible research with open-source code
- Interdisciplinary collaboration

**Student Authorship:**

PhD students are encouraged to be first authors on 3+ papers during their PhD. We support students through:

- Paper writing workshops
- Internal review before submission
- Conference presentation coaching
- Collaboration opportunities with clinical partners
