## On-device & efficient models

<div class="card-grid">
<div class="card">
<h3>SeLoRA</h3>
<p>Self-expanding low-rank adaptation that adapts a foundation model by training 8.5M of 850M parameters (~1%), recovering full-tuning quality for medical image synthesis at a fraction of the memory and compute.</p>
<p style="font-size:0.8rem;color:var(--ink-muted);"><em>Expert Systems with Applications (2026) · <a href="https://doi.org/10.1016/j.eswa.2026.131569">DOI</a> · <a href="https://arxiv.org/abs/2408.07196">arXiv</a> · <a href="https://yuchen20.github.io/SeLoRA.github.io/">project</a></em></p>
</div>
<div class="card">
<h3>Point-of-care intervention AI</h3>
<p>Sub-second path planning for coronary intervention, running intra-operatively on the procedure-room workstation rather than a remote server.</p>
<p style="font-size:0.8rem;color:var(--ink-muted);"><em>Computers in Biology &amp; Medicine (2023) · <a href="https://doi.org/10.1016/j.compbiomed.2023.107540">DOI</a></em></p>
</div>
</div>

## Federated & privacy-preserving learning

<div class="card-grid">
<div class="card">
<h3>Heterogeneous federated distillation</h3>
<p>Knowledge distillation that lets hospitals with different model architectures and hardware learn together — sharing knowledge, not patient data. The first demonstration of cross-architecture federated learning for medical imaging.</p>
<p style="font-size:0.8rem;color:var(--ink-muted);"><em>IEEE Trans. Industrial Informatics (2020) · IEEE Trans. Medical Imaging (2020)</em></p>
</div>
</div>

## Population-scale imaging & synthesis

<div class="card-grid">
<div class="card">
<h3>UK Biobank bone-marrow adiposity</h3>
<p>High-throughput deep learning over 39,000+ whole-body MRI, linking imaging phenotypes to cardiovascular and haematological disease through PheWAS and Mendelian randomization.</p>
<p style="font-size:0.8rem;color:var(--ink-muted);"><em>Nature Communications ×2 (2025) · <a href="https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-024-55422-4">DOI</a> · <a href="https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-025-63395-1">DOI</a></em></p>
</div>
<div class="card">
<h3>DiCyc — cross-domain synthesis</h3>
<p>GAN-based deformation-invariant cross-domain synthesis that bridges modalities and reconciles multi-centre acquisition differences without paired data.</p>
<p style="font-size:0.8rem;color:var(--ink-muted);"><em>Information Fusion (2021) · <a href="https://doi.org/10.1016/j.inffus.2020.10.015">DOI</a></em></p>
</div>
<div class="card">
<h3>SCOT-HEART plaque AI</h3>
<p>Automated coronary plaque quantification with cross-device robustness across 12 scanners, supporting risk stratification in a 4,146-patient multicentre trial.</p>
<p style="font-size:0.8rem;color:var(--ink-muted);"><em>Circulation (2020)</em></p>
</div>
</div>
